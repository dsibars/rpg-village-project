# Story Image Plan — RPG Village Chronicler's Journal

> **How to use this file:**
> 1. Each image has a **final filename** (e.g., `prologue_valley_dawn.webp`)
> 2. Each has a **generation prompt** for Stable Diffusion / Midjourney / DALL-E
> 3. All placeholders are already created in `public/assets/story/` — just override the file
> 4. Art style: **Hand-inked village journal sketches** — sepia-toned, slightly rough, like a villager with artistic skill drew them by candlelight

---

## Art Direction (Apply to ALL images)

**Style:** Hand-inked sketchbook illustration, sepia and brown ink tones, slightly rough linework, occasional ink splatters, as if drawn by a skilled but not professional village chronicler. Warm paper texture background. No photorealism — illustrative, evocative, personal.

**Color palette:** Sepia, burnt sienna, warm brown, parchment cream, occasional touches of dark green or muted gold for emphasis.

**Mood:** Personal, witnessed, slightly nostalgic, sometimes urgent. The chronicler draws what they see from their window, the ridge, the village square.

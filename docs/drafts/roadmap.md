# Project Roadmap & Drafts

This document contains "vague" definitions and brainstorming ideas for future modules. Items here are NOT yet specified or implemented. They can be "promoted" to full specifications when ready.

> **Note:** The Magic Circle system was previously listed here but has been fully implemented. See [Magic Circle System](../shared/combat/magic_circle_system.md) for the canonical specification.

---

## 📅 Village Calendar & Defense
- **Concept:** A visual calendar in the Village UI that shows upcoming "Events."
- **Invasion Events:** Some days are marked for "Incoming Attack."
- **Interaction**:
    - When clicking "Next Day" on a day with an invasion, the game triggers a defensive combat.
    - The player must assign heroes to "Defense Slots" before ending the day.
    - If the defense fails, resources or building levels might be lost.

## 🏆 Achievement / Milestone System
- **Concept:** Track global stats (Total gold gathered, total goblins slain) and provide unique rewards or titles.

## 🎭 Villager Personalities
- **Concept:** Give villagers unique traits (e.g., "Lazy," "Hardworking," "Glutton").
- **Impact:** Affects food consumption and production efficiency in assigned tasks.

Now, we have areas and expeditions with tree generation etc. This is fine, but we want to edit the "thematics" of the areas (check docs/explore folder)

As a Product Manager, think about how to make areas more thematic like:

- initial area (for tutorial, for unlocking the first 3-4 new heroes soon with expedition histories, etc)
- the woods area may enforce maybe fast enemies, and ensure looting of basic resources (stone, wood, herb)
- an area more focused for MAgic (and be easy to link with it). The main area to unlock glyphs for magic circle, and enemies that rely more on magic
- an area that drops advanced resources mostly, but their scalation between expeditions is hard (make heroes need some improvements to acces new expeditions easily...or collecting advancing resources may be too easy)


Think about more topics, but the idea is to plan more thematic areas, and diverse, and even relations to when to unlock

A point for me that will be interesting, is that the magic area will be available soon too... and also the 3rd hero to be a mage in the adventure (maybe on the initial areas, if you didn't built the building for magic, when you rescue the 3rd hero that is more wizard type, it gives you "free" the building if you don't have yet, and unlocks this "magic themed" area)


To feel it more, we need to think also to show history messages when advancing day (not only the resume one). Like when we start a new game that we have 3 pictures and a description, replicate it when a history expedition ended, to show it.
